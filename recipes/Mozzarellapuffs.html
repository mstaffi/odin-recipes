How to make Mozzarella Puffs

DESCRIPTION

Buttermilk biscuits are stuffed with mozzarella cheese, covered in pizza sauce and baked to golden perfection.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 (7.5 ounce) package refrigerated buttermilk biscuit dough
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 3 ounces mozzarella cheese, cut into 3/4 inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons pizza sauce

STEPS

  1. Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Step 2: Make an indentation in the top of each uncooked biscuit. Fill indentations with oregano and a mozzarella cube. Pinch the dough around the mozzarella cubes.
  3. Step 3: Place the biscuits, pinched side down, on a medium, ungreased baking sheet. Cover with pizza sauce.
  4. Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven 10 to 12 minutes, or until golden brown
